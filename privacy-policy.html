Privacy Policy

Grà — Relationship Reminders  ·  Last updated: March 2026

1. Overview

Grà is a personal reminder app that helps you stay in touch with the people you love. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app handles, where it is stored, and your rights.

2. Data we collect

Grà does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on our servers. All information you enter into the app — including names, relationship types, schedule preferences, and interaction logs — is stored exclusively on your device using your browser's local storage.

We do not have access to any of this information. It never leaves your device.

3. Push notifications

If you grant notification permission, Grà uses your device's built-in notification system to deliver reminders at times you have chosen. Notification scheduling is handled entirely on-device. No notification content is transmitted to any external server.

4. Affiliate links

The Gifts section of Grà contains links to products on Amazon. These are affiliate links, meaning if you make a purchase after clicking one, we may receive a small commission at no extra cost to you. Clicking these links takes you to Amazon's website, which has its own privacy policy. We do not share any personal data with Amazon in connection with these links.

5. Analytics and tracking

Grà does not use any analytics tools, tracking pixels, cookies, or third-party SDKs. We do not track how you use the app.

6. Children's privacy

Grà is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us at: privacy@gracreator.com
